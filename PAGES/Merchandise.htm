Brian has recorded and mixed many CDs for your dancing pleasure. These CDs are enjoyable to listen to and are useful for home square dance parties or in a hall when no local caller is available.

The choreography on Brian's CDs has a broad range of interesting moves and is fun to dance to, even at the basic level.

All of Brian's Square Dancing CDs have a fantastic atmosphere as they are recorded live at his dances and special events. The sound is of a high quality, has good balance and is easy to understand.

There are also:

  • Square Dancing CDs with levels including Basic, Mainstream, Plus, A1 and A2 all called by the versatile and charismatic Brian Hotchkies
  • Clogging CDs cued by the internationally respected Leanne Nicholson.
  • Round Dancing CDs cued by the pleasant voice of the great John Stallard
