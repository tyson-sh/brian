Itinerary

Brian runs many clubs throughout the month and often appears at functions and regular special weekends around the country.  All of Brian’s dances are filled with enjoyable, yet challenging and interesting dancing, friendly conversation, laughter, and often, eating!  The links below offer much more detail about such events.
Everyone is always welcome, so please come along and join us for a dance! We would love to see you there.
